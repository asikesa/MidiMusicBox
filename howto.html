MidiMusicBox

Turn midi files into printable scores for DIY music boxes

How To & About

  1. Edit your midi file to only contain the white keys between C1-C3
  2. Export your midi file, it can have any many tracks as you wish
  3. Select the file to convert and upload it
  4. Print the PDF out and overlay the tabs, applying a small piece of tape covering the overlap
  5. Punch out all the notes
  6. Crank away!
